Contrast

    Salt Lake City

This site is a great example of contrast. You get it in a couple of different ways. First of all, the yellow on black combines very well with the picture the colors are surrounding. Second, the black also contrasts with the white so the black doesn't dominate the scene.

Repetition

    SUU

I liked the SUU site as an example of repetition. There are two different sections that show this. The first is just below the SUU logo, we can the different links with the same color. Although icons next to them are alternating, it still shows the uniformity.

White Space

    Rotten Tomatoes

I chose the Rotten Tomatoes website for whitespace. On the other sites there were places where logos or ads overlapped one another, but this was not the case with Rotten Tomatoes. There is enough space between each element of the page, giving it a certain elegance.
