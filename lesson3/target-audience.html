Weather Reporter

weatherreporter.com

Target Audience

The target audience of Weather Reporter are the local people that live in the areas of Preston, Fish Haven, and Soda Springs. These individuals will be able to access this website to find information on the weather as well as events that may be happening in the area. It will also provide information to people who plan on visiting these areas for vacations. Those vacationing here will likely be in the middle class, averaging $50,000 per year and will likely range from single people to small families with 2-4 children. The services on this website will be useable from a mobile phone, tablet, or laptop.

Personas

  • Sharon and her husband like to travel. She is a few years older than her husband and has a degree in psychology. Her body temperature has always been different from that of her husband. While he can usually muscle through a chilly evening, she likes to check the weather to make sure she knows what temperatures to prepare for and how thick of a jacket to bring. She would use her phone to access the weather.

  • Mike and Stacy have been married for 12 years and have 4 children. The oldest one in 11 and the youngest one is five. They have already had situations arise from vacations in which one or more of the children have been sun-burned or lost their jacket. They now like to check the weather sites in the local areas they will be visiting to be sure that they have the necessary equipment for the trip. They might check the weather from their phone or from their laptop.

Scenarios

  1. If I plan a long hike today, what is the chance the weather will remain nice?
  2. It looks a bit overcast right now, will I need an umbrella to get around on foot?
  3. I'm going to visit a friend for a few days, will I need to bring sun-block?
  4. Is it warm enough for the children to play outside without jackets?
  5. It rained yesterday, will I be able to skip watering my lawn today as well?
  6. Will the weather be hot enough to require water bottles on the hike?
  7. My in-laws are coming in to town today, will I need to plan a day trip or will we be stuck inside?
